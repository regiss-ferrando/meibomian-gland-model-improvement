# MGD-1k Dataset: Selection & Justification

## Dataset Overview

**MGD-1k Dataset**: 1,000 infrared meibomian gland images with segmentation masks and graded meiboscore

### Official Links
- **Dataset Page**: https://mgd1k.github.io/
- **GitHub Repository**: https://github.com/MGD1K/MGD1k/tree/main/Expore%20MGD1k%20Dataset

### Source Paper
- **Title**: Automated quantification of meibomian gland dropout in infrared meibography using deep learning

## Why This Dataset?

1. **Task Clarity**: Dual objectives—gland segmentation and meiboscore (severity) prediction
2. **Clinical Relevance**: Addresses meibomian gland dysfunction (MGD), a leading cause of dry eye disease
3. **Annotation Quality**: Expert-labeled with medical validation
4. **Appropriate Size**: 1,000 images—sufficient for deep learning but manageable for detailed analysis
5. **Research Potential**: Published baseline results demonstrate room for meaningful improvement
6. **Accessibility**: Publicly available, no complex registration required
